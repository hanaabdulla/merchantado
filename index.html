Our Services

Merchantado provides best quality spices with strict quality control and testing.Merchantado also offers services that encompass sourcing, processing, quality assurance, distribution, and market insights.

Export and Import Services

Merchantado operate globally and provide export and import services. They handle all the necessary documentation, customs clearance, and logistics involved in shipping spices to different countries. This allows Merchantado to reach a wide range of customers worldwide.

Product development

Merchantado engage in product development, creating unique spice blends, seasonings, or customized formulations. They work closely with chefs, food manufacturers, and culinary experts to develop new flavors and cater to specific market demands.

processing and packaging

Merchantado ave their own processing facilities where they clean, sort, and process the spices. This may involve techniques such as grinding, blending, roasting, or toasting to enhance flavor and aroma. They also take care of proper packaging, ensuring that the spices are protected from moisture, light, and air, thereby maintaining their freshness.
