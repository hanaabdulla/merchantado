About Us

Merchantado: Your Gateway to Exquisite Spices

Merchantado is a reputable company based in Kannur, specializing in the export and import of high-quality spices. With a commitment to excellence and a passion for flavor, Merchantado has established itself as a trusted source for aromatic spices that enhance culinary experiences around the world.At Merchantado, we understand the importance of sourcing spices from the finest producers. We have built strong relationships with farmers and suppliers, ensuring that only the freshest and most flavorful spices make their way into our customers' kitchens. Our teamof experts meticulously selects and hand-picks each spice, adhering to strict quality standards and ensuring that every product meets our customers' expectations.As an exporter, Merchantado takes pride in showcasing the diverse range of spices available in the vibrant region of Kannur. From the warm and earthy notes of cumin to the fiery kick of red chili powder, we offer a wide selection of spices to suit various culinary preferences and cuisines. Our careful sourcing and packaging processes ensure that the rich flavors and aromas of these spices are preserved, maintaining their authenticity and quality throughout the journey to your doorstep.
